Project Overview

A face detection project involves the use of computer vision and image processing techniques to locate and identify human faces within images or video streams. The objective of such a project is to automatically detect and extract facial features, such as eyes, nose, and mouth, to understand the presence and position of human faces

  • Data Collection: Gather a dataset of images or video frames that contain human faces
  • Preprocessing: Prepare the images or video frames for analysis by applying preprocessing techniques
  • Face Detection Algorithm: Implement or employ a face detection algorithm to locate and identify faces within the images or video frames
  • We use yolov7 new state of the art real time object detection
  • Feature Extraction: After detecting faces, extract relevant facial features such as eyes, nose, and mouth
  • Application Development: Integrate the face detection capabilities into an application or system according to the project requirements
